My Passion

Ever since I was little, art has been my passion. It has been through art that I have dealt with emotions and certain aspects of my life. We are all created in Gods image, the first artist, and He has gifted us with creativity and skill to create art and glorify Him through it. 

My Path

I am currently studying at Bob Jones University as a Studio Art Major, with a focus on Illustration. I mostly work in digital, but my studies have pushed me to new mediums that I have grown to love and enjoy working in.

My Background

I am from Cape Town South Africa, but currently live in Easley South Carolina. I attend Covenant Reformed Baptist Church where my dad is the pastor.
